Tracey founded AQActive

with two goals in mind: first, to offer a variety of activewear for apple body shapes; and second, to empower women to embrace a balanced lifestyle minus the perfectionism social media portrays. Being a mother herself, who underwent her own challenges to adopt an active lifestyle, she is aware of the challenges new mums encounter and wanted to make a positive impact using all the tips, tricks and hacks she’s learned along her journey on how to balance motherhood with a healthy lifestyle.

Because of this, AQ Active strives to be a high-quality activewear company that empowers women to take small, consistent steps towards living a healthier, more balanced lifestyle.
